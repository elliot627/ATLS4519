What is Urban Exploration?

Urban exploration (often shortened as urbex or UE) is the exploration of man-made structures, usually abandoned ruins or not usually seen components of the man-made environment. Photography and historical interest/documentation are heavily featured in the hobby and, although it usually involves trespassing onto private property, this is not always the case and is of innocent intention.
